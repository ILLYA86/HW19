Top Popular Detailing Services

  • A Detailing Wash - is an important part of proper car care.

  • Polishing - is the mechanical removal of a damaged layer of material to restore its shine.

  • Dry cleaning - is an effective and gentle way of cleaning fabric, where the contamination is soaked in a special solvent, which is then extracted using an extractor.

  • Protective compositions - make the paintwork shine and protect it from the external environment.

  • Quartz protective compositions - give the paintwork an extraordinary caramel shine and a powerful hydrophobic effect.
